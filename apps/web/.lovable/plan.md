# Mobile cash flow view\n\n## The problem\n\nThe current desktop layout assumes a wide horizontal grid: 200px sticky label column + N×100px month columns, with a chart aligned pixel-perfect above. On a 393px phone you only see ~1.5 months at a time, the stat cards take half the screen, and the chart is squashed into the visible portion of the scroll container. Pinching, swiping a 200px-locked column, and tapping tiny cells is painful.\n\n**Responsive design alone is not enough here.** The desktop view is fundamentally a 2D spreadsheet — wide tables don't work on phones no matter how you tweak them.
